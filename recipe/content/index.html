Banana Bread

Description

Banana bread is a type of loaf made out of ripe banans. This is a perfect sweet treat or a quick breakfast option to make especially when your bananas start becoming brown. This recipe specifically uses greek yogurt as well as agave as an alternative to sugar, making it healthy yet delicious. The walnut cinammon crumble further gives it a cozy fall flavor that you don't want to miss out on.

Created by: Janice Pae

Time & Prep

Total Time: 58min.
Prep Time: 20 min.
Cook Time:38 min.

Recipe

Ingredients

Wet Ingredients
  • 1/2 cup of plain yogurt
  • 1 egg
  • 4T melted butter
  • 1/2 cup of agave
  • 1 banana > 2 if using agave
  • splash of vanilla extract
Dry Ingredients
  • 1 cup all purpose flour
  • 1/2 t baking soda
  • 3/4t baking powder
  • Pinch of Salt
  • Sift
  • Optional: chocolate chips

Equipment

  • Mixing Bowl
  • Spatula
  • Bread Pan
  • Sifter

Crumble

  • 1T soft unsalted buter
  • 1T of brown sugar
  • 1/4 cups of Walnuts / Oats
  • Cinnamon

Directions

  • Preheat the oven to 350F
  • Melt the butter
  • Mash the bananas
  • Mix the yogurt, egg, butter, agave, and vanilla into a bowl
  • In another bowl, mix flour, baking soda, baking powder and Salt
  • Shift the dry ingredients into the wet ingredients and mix well
  • optional: add in chocolate chips
  • Spray the bread pan with oil
  • Put it into a bread pan
  • Sprinkle the crumble
  • Bake for 38 minutes
